End-to-End Protocol

How It Works.

From vetting to payout — every step is tracked, protected, and transparent.

1

Pass the Entry Exam

Creator

Complete a short foundational quiz covering tea chemistry, terroir, and botanical knowledge. Score 70%+ to enter the network. Your score becomes your Match Rating visible to brands.

2

Build Your Profile

Creator

Link your Instagram and TikTok via API for verified audience metrics. Complete Pro Academy modules to earn specialist badges (Chemistry, Terroir, Botanical, Gongfu, Sourcing). Upload up to 5 showcase reels. Your identity stays anonymized — brands see badges, metrics, and content quality, never your name.

3

Post a Campaign Brief

Brand

Define deliverables, select required badges, set content format (Reels, TikTok, YouTube, photography), and lock your budget into escrow. Funds are held by the platform — not the creator — until you approve final content.

4

Algorithmic Matching

Platform

TeaTrade's matching engine scores creators against your brief requirements — badge overlap, audience demographics, engagement rate trajectory, and content style. You see a ranked shortlist with full KPI dashboards and video previews.

5

Invite & Ship Product

Brand

Select your preferred creators and send invites. Once accepted, the creator shares a secure delivery address (visible only for this campaign). Ship your product with a tracking number. The creator confirms receipt — the 7-day content creation clock starts automatically.

6

7-Day Content Window

Creator

A live countdown begins from the moment product is confirmed received. The creator produces content according to the brief specs. They upload final assets directly through the platform before the deadline. Late submissions trigger an automatic protocol review.

7

24-Hour Review Window

Brand

Once content is submitted, the brand has exactly 24 hours to review. Accept to release payment instantly. Reject only if content contains factual inaccuracies or offensive material. If 24 hours pass with no action, payment auto-releases to protect the creator.

8

Escrow Release & Payout

Platform

On approval, funds release immediately from escrow to the creator. A 12% platform fee is deducted transparently. If rejected, the TeaTrade dispute protocol activates — an independent review within 48 hours. No one loses money unfairly.
