FAQ

Common Questions.

How are creators vetted?
Every creator passes a technical entry exam covering tea chemistry, terroir, and botanical knowledge. They must score 70%+ to enter the network. Social metrics are API-verified directly from Instagram and TikTok — no self-reported numbers.
What does escrow protection mean?
Your campaign budget is held securely in escrow and only released to the creator once you’ve approved the deliverables. If content doesn’t meet the brief, funds stay protected. Full transparency, zero risk.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Growth plans can be cancelled anytime — no lock-in. Enterprise partnerships run on a quarterly basis. Any briefs already in progress at cancellation will be completed and protected by escrow as normal.
How is this different from a traditional agency?
Traditional agencies charge 15–30% markups and monthly retainers regardless of output. TeaTrade is performance-first: you only pay for briefs posted, and every creator’s expertise is exam-certified. No account managers upselling you, no inflated metrics — just verified, niche talent matched by algorithm.
What content formats can creators deliver?
Short-form video (Reels, TikTok), long-form YouTube reviews, story series, static photography, and written blog content. Each brief specifies the exact deliverables, formats, and dimensions required.
How fast will I receive content?
Standard turnaround is 14 days from brief acceptance. Enterprise clients with exclusive creator locks can negotiate expedited timelines. Most creators deliver initial drafts within 7–10 days.
